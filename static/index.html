Legends tell that deep in these frozen caverns, crystals lie scattered about the icy halls. Ancient and mysterious, these crystals will grant immense power to the one who can gather them all together.

Needless to say, the caverns are the subject of much interest to students at the local arcane university. Whether the rumors are true, or merely the result of upperclassmen pranking the freshman, every new year sees a multitude of apprentice mages descending into the caves.

Those that search are known as...

Flamethrowing Dynamite Wizards on Ice

"Flamethrowing," largely because it's the first spell taught at the academy. "Dynamite" because flamethrowing doesn't always quite cut it. Donning ice skates, these wizards explore the caves and gather up crystals. Since the legends say "the one" who gathers all the crystals, and not "the team," cooperation is infrequently the norm.

Instructions

  • WASD skates around.
  • Left mouse throws flames.
  • Right mouse throws dynamite (hold button to adjust fuse length).
  • Gather all the crystals to win!
